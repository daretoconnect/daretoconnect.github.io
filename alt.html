Learn how to lead from your true self
Authentic leadership training
Stockholm, May 30-Jun 1
 

Our truth is our greatest asset.
So why don’t we lead from it more often?

This training will deepen your leadership skills just by showing more of yourself.

You will learn to

Create psychological safety for your team
Connect easily to get the best out of your relationships
Address and transform difficulties to release tensions
Holding safe space to allow vulnerability
Keeping integrity when challenged

 

"This training brought out the inner badass leader in me"

The core of this training is Authentic Relating and Circling. These practices have been proven to create distinct shifts in communication skill, empathetic awareness, and authentic expression even within a few days.



 
 

Sara Ness

Sara Ness has worked with over 1 000 leaders in sectors ranging from Google to the Coast Guard on the art of authentic leadership. Sara is passionate about exposing the joy of human connection and exploring our creative potential through relationship. Her writings and teaching have led to the foundation of 80+ Authentic Relating and Circling communities. Sara is the founder and CEO of Authentic Revolution, and her publications - including The Authentic Relating Games Manual, the core text of the Authentic Relating field - have helped seed 100+ communities around the world.
