What is Dare to Connect?

To us, relationships are an ongoing process created by two or more people together. For such a process to work smoothly, we need skills and practice. Most of us didn't learn this in school, so we struggle to build and maintain relationships, whether they are romantic, professional or with family members. Dare to Connect is a practice to strengthen your relationship skills in all areas.
